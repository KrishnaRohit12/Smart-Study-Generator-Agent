Foundation Model

Smart Study Generator is built on IBM's Granite 3.3 8B open-source language model, accessed through watsonx.ai. With Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), the model grounds every answer strictly in your uploaded content — providing accurate, hallucination-resistant responses you can trust for exam preparation.
