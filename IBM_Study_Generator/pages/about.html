About Smart Study Generator

An AI-powered academic companion built on IBM Granite, designed to transform how students learn.

Problem Statement

The Study Challenge

Students today are overwhelmed with scattered study materials spread across lectures, PDFs, handwritten notes, and online resources. Without a centralised system, managing and extracting key knowledge becomes a time-consuming burden rather than a learning exercise.

There is no feedback loop — students cannot easily identify which topics they understand well and which require further revision. The lack of personalisation in traditional study tools means every student follows the same path regardless of their individual strengths and weaknesses.

Scattered Notes
Materials spread across multiple platforms with no unified access
No Personalisation
One-size-fits-all study resources ignore individual learning gaps
No Feedback Loop
No mechanism to track which topics need more attention
Information Overload
Dense academic content with no intelligent summarisation
Our Solution

One Platform, Powered by IBM Granite AI

A seamless three-step pipeline that transforms raw study material into personalised, actionable learning experiences.

Upload & Extract

Students upload PDFs, DOCX, or plain text notes. The platform extracts raw content and structures it into a knowledge base ready for AI processing.

AI Processing

IBM Granite 3.3 analyses the content using Retrieval-Augmented Generation, identifies key concepts, and builds personalised quizzes, flashcards, and study plans.

Personalised Output

The student receives AI-generated study aids tailored to their uploaded material — quizzes, flashcard decks, study schedules, and a conversational AI tutor.

Technology

IBM Granite 3.3 Foundation Model

Enterprise-grade, responsible AI built for real-world academic and business applications, backed by IBM's commitment to transparency.

IBM Granite 3.3 is a family of open, performant, and enterprise-ready foundation models designed for text generation, summarisation, classification, and reasoning tasks. The 8B Instruct variant powering this platform delivers accurate, contextually-aware responses tailored to academic content.

Deep Context Understanding
128K token context window enables analysis of entire textbooks and multi-chapter documents in a single pass.
RAG-Enabled Retrieval
Retrieval-Augmented Generation grounds every AI response directly in the student's own uploaded notes, eliminating irrelevant answers.
Academic Language Precision
Fine-tuned for structured academic output — quiz generation, flashcard formatting, and study plan scheduling are all first-class capabilities.
Responsible AI by Design
IBM Granite models are trained with transparency and safety guidelines, with no third-party data leakage or undisclosed training sources.

Platform Features

What the Platform Does

Six powerful AI-driven tools, all working from your own study material.

Smart Note Upload
Upload PDFs, DOCX files, or paste raw text and the AI extracts structured content instantly. Supports multi-document sessions so you can combine course materials from different sources.
AI Chat Tutor
An IBM Granite-powered conversational assistant answers questions directly from your uploaded notes using RAG. Ask anything from concept clarification to exam-style questions.
AI Quiz Generator
Automatically generates multiple-choice and short-answer quizzes from your notes. Each question is grounded in uploaded content, with instant scoring and topic-based feedback.
Flashcard Decks
IBM Granite extracts key terms and definitions to generate spaced-repetition flashcard decks. Flip cards, mark confidence levels, and track mastery across each subject.
Study Planner
The AI generates a personalised day-by-day study schedule based on your exam dates and uploaded subjects. Balances workload intelligently across available days.
Progress Dashboard
Visual analytics track study hours, quiz scores, flashcard mastery, and weak topics over time. Charts and goal-tracking keep motivation high and revision targeted.

Agentic AI

What is Agentic AI?

Unlike a static chatbot that simply responds to prompts, an agentic AI system perceives its environment, reasons over available information, and takes deliberate actions to achieve a goal. Smart Study Generator operates as an intelligent agent — it observes what you upload, reasons over the content using IBM Granite, and autonomously generates tailored study resources.

1
Perceive
The agent receives uploaded notes, current quiz scores, and session context as its environment input.
2
Reason
IBM Granite analyses the content, identifies knowledge gaps, and determines the best study interventions.
3
Act
The agent generates quizzes, flashcards, study plans, and chat responses — all grounded in the student's own material.

IBM University Engagement Programme

IBM University Engagement Project

Built on IBM Granite AI, IBM Cloud, and watsonx.ai, this project was developed during the 2024–2025 academic year as a practical demonstration of agentic AI in higher education. The platform showcases how IBM's enterprise AI stack can be applied to solve real student challenges.

"This project was developed as part of IBM University Engagement, demonstrating the application of IBM Granite foundation models in academic settings — bridging enterprise AI capabilities with the everyday needs of students."

The project covers the full AI development lifecycle: data ingestion, RAG pipeline construction, foundation model integration via watsonx.ai, front-end delivery, and continuous evaluation — all aligned with IBM's responsible AI principles.
